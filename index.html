Our Vision

What we've done:

We’re Back in SEASon, a party composed of incumbent ESC members. In the past, we have collaborated with student organizations and faculty to approve key policies for our constituents and hold events to boost our campus spirit! Some of our favorite ones were: creating SEAS Gala, initiating Explore NYC, organizing Meet a Fellow Engineer, and passing … the Fall 2021 P/D/F!

What we'll do:

  1. Policies:
    • P/D/F: Advocacy for a PERMANENT P/D/F policy, allowing SEAS students two floating P/D/Fs to be used on any course in any semester.
    • Student Metrocard Initiative: SEAS students will have access to NYC by means of free MetroCards provided by ESC.
    • Transfer Inclusion Initiative: Organization of a special NSOP, exclusive acceptance packages, and a transfer-specific days on campus.
    • Mental/General Health Initiatives: Communication with administrators to improve mental health services and accessibility.
  2. Events you will actually go to: Rooftop formal (Bridgerton-style ball), SEAS Gala Cruise - 2023 Edition, Explore NYC Initiative 2.0, puppy day, cultural fairs, and lots and lots of free food!
  3. DEI: We are committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion with forward-thinking policies like an FGLI fundraising initiative, mentorship programs, support for minority and cultural organizations.

Thank you, and we're so excited for us to thrive together.

-Gabe, Shomik, Matt, Chloe, and Angel
