Our Vision

What We've Done:

We’re the TrustSEAS, a party made up of five dedicated ESC members who have been essential to this past year’s SEAS Cruise, Fall Formal, ExploreNYC, and FREE Merch drops alongside P/D/F policy, mental health initiatives, and expanding dining options!

What We'll Do:

  1. P/D/F: We’ll fight to expand P/D/F to include a more diverse range of courses—both technical AND non-technical—so you can expand your intellectual horizons, free of GPA concerns!
  2. MetroCard Initiative: In addition to free metrocards for FLI students, we are working towards a collaboration with the MTA to get FREE transport for all undergrads.
  3. DEI: Uplift affinity groups and program leaders to improve cross-organization collaboration and support. Collaborate with Reproductive Health Justice Collaborative to increase access to abortion medication/essential healthcare on campus!
  4. Dining: We envision 24-hour JJ’s, dining halls with open seats, and food options that accommodate students of all dietary needs.
  5. Housing: We’ll advocate for flexible summer housing, thorough sanitation, and air conditioning in every residence hall.
  6. Health: Improve accessibility of physical and mental health resources.
  7. Events: Beyond our existing traditions, we’ll bring more free food, cultural fairs, and host a SEAS formal in one of NYC’s beautiful museums!

Thank you, and we're so excited for us to thrive together.

-HaYoung, Kavi, Rachinta, Janie, and Gabe
